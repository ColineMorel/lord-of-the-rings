Mordor

Mordor, litterally land of shadow, is a region of Middle Earth owned by the Dark Lord Sauron. It is borded by mountains, and very hard to penetrate.

The volcano of Mordor, Mont Doom, is probably the most important location in all the trilogy, because it is where the ring of power was forged and the only place it can be destroyed.

This is the place where Frodo and Sam are headed, and the place they will end their quest.At the end of the movie, we witness the destruction of Mordor, by the explosion and collapse of the tomer of the eye of Sauron.
