Helm's Deep

Helm's deep was a large valley located in the north-west of the White Mountains. The Keep was originally an encampment of Rohirrim.

In the movie trilogy the fortress will become the refuge of the inhabitants of Edoras, trying to protect themselves against the attacks of the Uruk-Hais of Sarumane.

Unfortunately, in the adaptation, the only weakness of Helm's Deep Fortress will be revealed by Grima, former councelor of Theoden King, to Saruman, which will lead to the blowing of the wall of the courtyard and the entrance of the armies of Isengard.
