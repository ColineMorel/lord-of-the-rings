The Mines of Moria

The Mines of Moria, called Khazad-Dum by the Dwarves, are an enormous underground complex. It is filled with Mithril.

During the Third age it has been taken over by Orcs and is also inhabited by a demon of the Ancient World : a Balrog.

Stopped in the mountains by the powers of Sarumane, the Fellowship of The Ring will have to risk taking the path of Moria in order to cross the mountain.

This place will be the location of the transformation of Gandlf The Grey into Gandalf The White.
