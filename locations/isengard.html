Isengard

Isengard was built during the Second Age of Middle Earth. It is located in the west-end corner of the Kingdom of Rohan.

Taking advantage of Sauron's Return, Sarumane became the Lord of Isengard in 2953. It then was used as a base for Sarumane to destroy the Rohirrims.

It was destroyed during the War of The Ring by an army of Ents and then restored during the Fourth Age and given to Treebeard.
