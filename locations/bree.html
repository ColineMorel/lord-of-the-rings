Bree

In the Lord of The Rings Movie Trilogy, Bree only appears in the Fellowship Of The Ring, but still is an important place. Located in the east of the Shire, the Hobbits where originally supposed to meet Gandalf at the Inn of the Prancing Pony.

It was originally founded by the Men Of Twilight, and therefore is a village of men.

Instead of meeting Gandalf, who is prisonner of Saroumane at that time, the four Hobbits will encounter Strider who will help them escape the Black Riders, after Frodo accidentally wears the Ring for the first time.
