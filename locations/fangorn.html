Fangorn Forest

Fangorn Forest is located close from both the Gap of Rohan and Isengard. It is the territory of the Ents, shepard of the trees.

The forest has been damaged a lot of time during the ages of Middle Earth, mostly by Sarumane. It is that damage that will force the Ents to take part in the war by destroying Isengard and sending the trees as a help to men during the battle of Helm's Deep.
