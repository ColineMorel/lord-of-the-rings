Lothlorien

The forest of Lothlorien is one of the four forest inhabiting the wooden elves and as described by Tolkien, the fairest of them most likely because of the ring of power owned by Galadriel that helped the land prosper.

In 1200 of the Second Age , Galadriel planted the trees that would later be the foundation of Lothlorien.

It is located in the east of the Mines of Moria and will be the refuge of the Fellowship after their crossing of the Mines.
