Minas Morgul

Minas Morgul, also known as the Dead City is located at the edge of Mordor. It declined during the Third Age of Middle Earth, while The Kingdom of Gondor was rising.

The city inhabits the Wizard-King of Angmar as well as the Nazgul and in the movie will give the signal of the beginning of the war. Armies of Mordor will leave from here, marching upon Minas Tirith.
