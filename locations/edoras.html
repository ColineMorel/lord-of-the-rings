Edoras

Edoras is located on a hill in the Valley of the White Mountain in the kingdom of Rohan. It was not the first capital of Rohan: Aldburg was. But since Rohan consists mostly in small villages and farms, Edoras is actually the only really city of the Kingdom.

After leaving Fangorn's Forest, it is where Aragorn, Legolas, Gimli and Gandalf will head to in order to free King Theoden from a spell that makes him the puppet of Sarumane.
