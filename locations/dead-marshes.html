Dead Marshes

The Dead Marshes are described in the second volume of the Trilogy, but it is not the first time the spectator sees them. Indeed, it was already featured in one of the very first scenes of the movie during the Battle of Dargolad when the Last Alliance of men and elves defeated the forces of Mordor and Sauron.

They are located north-west of the principal entrance of Mordor, and are the path that Frodo and Samwise, lead by Gollum, will take in order to try to enter Mordor.
