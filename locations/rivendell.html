Rivendell

Rivendell is located at the edge of the river Bruinen. It was founded during the Second Age of Middle Earth by Elrond.

Apart from being the home of Elrond and his family, Rivendell played a very important part in the movie trilogy since it is in that location that the Fellowship of The Ring will be created and depart to Mordor.
