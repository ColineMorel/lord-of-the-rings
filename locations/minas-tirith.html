Minas Tirith

Minas Tirith, also called the White City, is the Capital of the Kingdom of Gondor. It was originally built to protect the former capital, Osgilliath.

A long description was made by J. R. R. Tolkien : the city was constituted by 7 floors and only vulnerable to earthquakes.

In the movie, the White City holds a very important role since it is the set of the attack of Mordor against Gondor.

Is is ruled by Denethor, steward and father of Boromir and Faramir. Legend has it that the tree of kings, located on the last floor of Minas Tirith, will bloom again when the king will return.
