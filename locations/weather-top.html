Weathertop

The ruins of the fortress of Amon-Sul, on top of Weathertop Hill, were once a tall and fair watch-tour.

Located almost halfway between the Shire and Rivendell, this fortress will be the set of scene of action in the movie trilogy: it will be the shelter of Strider and the hobbits right after they fled from Bree. Unfortunately, they are still chased by the Nazguls, and a battle will take place there between the 5 companions and the sevitors of Sauron.
