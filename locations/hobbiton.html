Hobbiton

Hobbiton is one of the small Hobbit village located approximatively in the middle of the Shire.

It is the home of Frodo, Bilbo, Sam, Merry and Pippin.

In the movie adaptation, we don't see it but Hobbiton suffered greatly from the War of The Ring and was partly destroyed by the armies of Sarumane.

It was then healed by a magic soil given by Galadriel to Sam.
