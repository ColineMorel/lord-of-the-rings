Gimli

Gimli is a member of the Durin's Folks and the only dwarf who volunteered to accompany Frodo in his quest.

He was the son of Gloin, one of the former companion of Bilbo.

After the Fellowship fell apart, he still stayed with Legolas and Aragorn throughout the entire War of the Ring , from Minas Tirith to Mordor.

In the movie trilogy, Gimli is played by John Rhys-Davis.
