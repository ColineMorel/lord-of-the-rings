Nazguls

The Nazguls, also called Black Riders or the Nines, are the Nine men who succombed to the power of the ring and became Sauron's servants.

They were once mortal men before Sauron gave them the rings of power.

They are chasing for the ring and will encounter Frodo and the Fellowship many times during the war.

Witch King of Angmar

One of the Nazguls, the most powerful of them, is the Witch King of Angmar. He is the one who stabbed Frodo on Weathertop.

He will be defeated and killed by Eowyn during the battle of Minas Tirith.
