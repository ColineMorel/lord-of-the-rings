Sauron

Sauron was at the origin of the forging of the great rings of power and the master of the One Ring. "One ring to rule them all, one ring to find them, one ring to bring them all and in the darkness bind them".

But the people of Middle Earth resisted his powers and during the War of The Last Alliance he was defeated, only to return during the Third Age of Middle Earth and try to win it over one more time.

His life is tightly bonded to the existence of the ring, that is why the ring must be destroyed in order for him to die.

Gollum

Originally named Smeagol, he was one of the River Folks, not so different from a Hobbit. But after he became in possession of the One Ring, he was exiled into the mountain, never to return. He forgot his name and was then called Gollum.

He lost the ring to Bilbo and sought for it all his life.

When he finally found it in possesion of Frodo, he became Sam and Frodo's guide to Mordor, still torn into the two parts of his personnality : Smeagol and Gollum.

His voice and gestures are played by Andy Serkis.
