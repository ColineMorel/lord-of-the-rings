Uruk-Hai

The Uruk Hai are a special breed of Orcs, really strong and capable to move in the sun.

Two kinds of Uruk Hais exist: the ones of Mordor and the ones of Sarumane.

Sarumane was suspected to have crossed them with men, making them stronger. They will become Sarumane's army against the people of Rohan and will wear the white hand of Sarumane as a sign of recognition.

Despite their strength, they will be defeated during the battle of Helm's Deep.

Orcs

Orcs are humanoid creatures, usually smaller than human size and regularly depicted as brutish, agressive and repulsive.

It is one of the peoples of Middle Earth and during the Third Age of Middle Earth, they will be Sauron's army against Gondor.

Unfortunately, a lot of them will die when the Dark Lord will be destroyed, making everything collapse around him.
