Legolas

Legolas is one of the wooden elves and son of Thranduil the king of Woodland.

He originally came to Rivendell to discuss Gollum's escape but was then chosen to go and be part of the Fellowship of The Ring.

He is armed with a bow and a long knife and will fight all the battles of the War of The Ring along with Aragorn, Gandalf and Gimli.

In the movie adaptation he is played by the world famous Orlando Bloom.

Galadriel

Galadriel is one of the royal elf gifted with a ring of power. She is described by Tolkien as "the mightiest and the fairest of all the elves that remained in Middle-Earth".

In the adaptation she is the one talking throughout the movie. And thanks to her gift of forsight, she will be able to help the members of the fellowship.

When they stopped by Lothlorien , she gave each of them a gift that they will need at one point during the wars.

She leave Middle Earth for the immortal land, when peace was restored in Middle Earth.

She is played by Cate Blanchett, also seen in Benjamin Button.

Elrond

Elrond was one of the last Lord of The Elves to have stayed in Middle Earth during its Third Age. He is the Lord of Rivendell.

Throughout his love for his daughter, he will force Aragorn to embrace his destiny and help gain the war by reforging the sword of Isildur with the shards of Narsil.

Hugo Weaving played his part in the movie adaptation.

Arwen

Arwen Undomiel is the daughter of Elrond and grand-daughter of Galadriel.

She is played by Liv Tyler and through her love story with Aragorn, she plays a major part in the setting of the story. Not only she saves Frodo, wounded by a morgul blade, but she decides to abandon her immmortal life to become Queen of Gondor after peace is back on Middle-Earth.
